Our Mission

To expose millions of LoL players to statistics concepts by showing how data can be used to answer questions about the game. We are doing this in the form of an awesome online experience that includes articles, graphics, and interactive elements.



Meet the Team

We are a team of passionate NCSU undergraduates and mentors working together to analyze League of Legends data in totally new ways!
